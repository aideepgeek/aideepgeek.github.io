## What is Performance Advertising?

Performance Advertising is the science of deploying capital into paid media channels to drive measurable business actions. In today's landscape, successful advertising is no longer just about creative elements; it is about signal quality, machine learning platform feedback loops, and accurate multi-touch attribution.

## Why Signal Quality & Algorithms Matter

* Up to 40% of ad signals are dropped or blocked by iOS privacy changes and traditional ad blockers.
* Over 70% of ad waste is driven by bad bidding signals feeding the ad platform's machine learning incorrect conversion data.
* Server-side tracking (CAPI) improves target accuracy by up to 20%, resulting in lower acquisition costs.
* Accurate attribution tracking allows companies to scale winning campaigns with confidence instead of guessing which channel worked.
