I create beautiful, functional websites that help businesses achieve their goals. My design process is user-centered, ensuring that every decision is backed by research and testing.

## What Makes My Design Different

### User-First Approach
Every design decision is made with your users in mind. I conduct thorough research to understand their needs, pain points, and behaviors.

### Business-Focused
While aesthetics are important, I never lose sight of your business objectives. Every element is designed to drive conversions and achieve your goals.

### Future-Proof
I design with scalability in mind. As your business grows, your website can easily adapt and expand.

## My Design Philosophy

1. **Simplicity is Key**: Clean, uncluttered designs that communicate clearly
2. **Consistency Matters**: Cohesive visual language across all pages
3. **Details Make Perfection**: Attention to micro-interactions and small details
4. **Accessibility for All**: Designs that work for everyone, regardless of ability

## Tools I Use

- **Figma** - Primary design tool
- **Adobe Creative Suite** - For complex graphics and illustrations
- **Sketch** - When working with macOS-focused teams
- **Principle** - For advanced prototyping and animations
