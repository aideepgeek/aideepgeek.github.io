## What is Enterprise GenAI?
Enterprise GenAI is not about asking ChatGPT questions; it is about building reliable, deterministic software systems wrapped around non-deterministic language models. True business value comes from building autonomous agents that possess advanced reasoning, deep industry context, long-term memory, and the precise ability to execute real-world APIs safely.
